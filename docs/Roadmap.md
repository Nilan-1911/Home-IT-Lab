# Roadmap

## Overview

The Home IT Lab is being developed incrementally through versioned milestones. Each milestone introduces new technologies, infrastructure components, or operational concepts while documenting the deployment process, configuration, troubleshooting, and lessons learned.

This roadmap provides a clear view of the project's current progress and planned future development.

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal of the Home IT Lab is to build a well-documented, production-inspired infrastructure that demonstrates practical Linux system administration, containerization, networking, automation, monitoring, and troubleshooting skills.

Rather than serving as a single completed project, the Home IT Lab is intended to evolve continuously as new technologies, services, and operational practices are explored and integrated.
